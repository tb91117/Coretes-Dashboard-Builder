# Platform Creative & Policy Constraints

## Google Ads (Performance Max)

- **Headline**: Max 30 characters
- **Long headline**: Max 90 characters
- **Description**: Max 90 characters
- **Images**: Square (1:1) or landscape (1.91:1), min 1200x628
- **Logo**: Square, 128x128 minimum
- **Bidding**: Maximize conversion value, Maximize conversions supported

## Meta (Shopping / Catalog Sales)

- **Primary text**: Max 125 characters for short format
- **Headline**: Max 40 characters
- **Call-to-action**: Shop now, Learn more
- **Images**: 1:1 square recommended for catalog
- **Audience**: Advantage+ eligible with broad targeting

## Amazon (Sponsored Brands)

- **Headline**: Max 50 characters
- **Brand name**: Displayed with logo
- **Images**: Logo + lifestyle image, 1200x628 landscape
- **Keywords**: From targeting hints, phrase/exact match
